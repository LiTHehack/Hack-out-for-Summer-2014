What

'Hack out for Summer 2014' is an initiative by Spotify Street Team and LiTHehack. It is a 100% non-profit event by students for students. It is also 100% free to participate. All we ask from you is a happy mode and an interest for concept, design and/or tech.

We want all students to be able to participate in this great day of innovation, inspiration and learning. Therefore we are competing in 3 categories, You absolutely do not have to be Zlatan at programming to join.

Our aim with this, except having fun, is to introduce you to the tools and possibilites that are right ou there waiting for you to build cool stuff, you just haven't started because you didn't know they existed. Sp make an investment in yourself, come and hack with us and find new cool areas connected to what you study, Then go nuts on cool projects over the summer. And hopefully you'll come back in September more motivated than ever.

How

The day starts off with 3 small inspiring talks related to the hack.
Innovationskontoret will talk about cool stuff about inovative ideas and stuff like that
Some awesome design person will tell you about how to make your design truly awesome.
LiTHehack will talk about techhy stuff and introduce the templates we've made available to you during the hack

Start hacking! Alot of genious will be closely available for you to talk to your ideas and give you some hints and trick about stuff. we will prepare a template for you to start working from if you wish so you can focus on your app-idea directly

You'll hold a quick presentation about your results for a jury consisting of supercool people within the many industries and with a lot of knowledge about design, programming and crossfunction

The jury will give you some feedback about your work and tips to make it even more the most ultimate app for the summer.

The jury will pick X projects within the field of Tech - Design - Idea that will win some amazing prices.

The party starts at "Visualiseringcenter C" with some snacks and beer and later head off to trappan and party with the rest of the students at Campus Norrköping

Where

During the day we will be in the amazing spaces of Strykbrädan. With whiteboard tbales, big windows towards the river and lots of space for the team. We think it is the best location possible to host this.

During the evening we will be in the bistro of visualiseringscenter C. We will wine, dine and enjoy each others presentations and company.

If you are up for dancing the night away, we will head to Trappan's closing night "Töm & Glöm" to properly welcome summer.
