What happened?

So, what happened the 24th of May, 2014?

Thanks to our magnificent partners, 40 hackers got together and created ten summer apps in varying shapes and forms. No matter the embodiment, concept or full-grown prototype, we're all very proud of what was achieved during these eight hours.

Without the help of our partners – Gaia, Yooba, Visualiceringscenter C, Norrköping Sience Park, Demola East Sweden, InnovationskontorEtt, Ida design and MT-sektionen – the hack would not have been possible. Also, big thanks to Moa Yngve, Ida Lilja, Lina Karlsson, P-O Jernberg and Paolo Brolin Echeverria who all did a wonderful job selecting the day's winners.

We hope you had at least half as fun as we did, because we had a blast.

Green-purple love,
Spotify Street Team LiU and LiTHehack
