Are you ready to Hack Out For Summer?

Are you ready to build the ultimate summer app?

Join us at Norrköping's very first hackathon organized by students

What

'Hack out for Summer 2014' is an initiative by Spotify Street Team and LiTHehack. It is a 100% non-profit event by students for students. It is also 100% free to participate. All we ask from you is a happy mode and a passion for concept, design and/or tech.

Our goal is for all students to be able to participate. Therefore you can compete in 3 categories: Concept, Design & Tech. In other words, you do not have to be Zlatan at programming to join hofs2014.

Another goal with this event is to introduce you to the tools and possibilites which are right there waiting for you to build cool stuff, you just haven't started because you didn't know they existed yet. So make an investment in yourself, come and hack with us and find new cool areas connected to what you study. Then go nuts on projects over the summer. And hopefully you'll come back in September more motivated than ever.

How

The day starts off with 3 inspiring talks related to the categories Concept, Design and Tech. Each talk is only 20 minutes and will hopefully create an "Can't wait to get started" feeling among you and your team mates.

Start hacking! The task is to build the ultimate summer app. We have invited an intresting mix of people which will be around for discusing ideas, giving advise and feedback during the day. LiTheHack has prepared a template for your team to start working from (if you wish) so you can focus on your app-idea directly.

You'll hold a short presentation showing your solution and pitch your idea to a jury consisting of supercool intelligente people.

The jury will give you feedback based on your idea and result. Was there anything missing for it to become the ultimate summer app?

The jury will pick one team within each category who presented the best solution. The winning teams will of course recive some magic epic prices.

The party starts at "Visualiseringcenter C" with dinner and beers. If you are up for dancing the night away, we will head to Trappan's closing night "Töm & Glöm" to properly welcome summer.

Registration

We recommend teams of 3-5 people, preferably from different programs with different skill sets. You'll need a cross functional team to build the most epic summer app, right? Pick one person responsible for the communication with us and let that person sign your team up.

Don't have a team but are really keen to join hofs2014? Post on the event's Facebook wall and we'll try to mix & match to put you in a great team.
