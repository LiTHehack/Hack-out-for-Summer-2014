Are you read to Hack Out For Summer?

Join us at Campus Norrköping and hack away

'Hack out for Summer' is all about having one last, great hackaton before we take a vacation from our keyboards and head out into the summer sun to relax and think of something else for a while.

But, it's also meant to prepare you for the summer... (More information to come soon!)

The hack will take place in "Färgeriet" in Campus Norrköping. The day is finished off at Visualiseringscenter C with Prize ceremony with beers and a buffé

We will then head for "Trappan" and party all night long at 'Töm & Glöm'
