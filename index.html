Norrköping 24 maj 2014

Are you read to Hack Out For Summer?

Join us at Campus Norrköping and hack away

'Hack out for Summer 2014' is all about having one last, great hackaton before we take a vacation from our keyboards and head out into the summer sun to relax and think of something else for a while.

But, it's also meant to prepare you for the summer.

The goal of the hackaton is to design the ultimate app to make this summer the best summer in your life

The hack will take place in "Strykbrädan" in Norrköping. The day will be finished off at Visualiseringscenter C with Prize ceremony and partying at Trappan

Who Can participate?

Anyone! This hack is for everyone, You don't even have to be a hacker or even know how to program - it's all about the idea and how you present it. There will be a prize within three categories: Idea, Technical Solution and Design
